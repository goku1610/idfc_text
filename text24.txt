Vyapaar Vriddhi Loan
====================

Small and micro enterprises generate substantial income and employment for rural households. However, they have limited access to resources from the banking and financial sectors, which could help them in upgrading their business. IDFC FIRST Bank **micro-enterprise loan** is offered to small and micro-enterprises. It can be applied for, from our nearest branch. This **small business loan** comes with one of the best MSME loan interest rates compared to informal financing sources. It offers some amazing benefits like borrowing with zero collaterals and doorstep collection.

One of the best features is that it requires no income proof for a loan up to ₹3 lakhs. This loan is now available in 100% paperless mode with the Instant Disbursal Option. Hence, IDFC FIRST Bank offers just the right **micro-enterprise loan** if you’re looking to expand your small/ micro-enterprise.
