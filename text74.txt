## Positive Pay System (PPS)

IDFC FIRST Bank prioritizes your safety and ease of banking.  Positive Pay safeguards your cheque payments, protecting you from tampering and potential fraud.

**How to use Positive Pay:**

1. **Start Now:** [https://my.idfcfirstbank.com/login](https://my.idfcfirstbank.com/login)
2. Log in to the IDFC FIRST Bank App or Netbanking.
3. Go to the cheque book management section.
4. Click on "new cheque details".
5. Fill in the following details:
   * Cheque Serial Number
   * Enter Payee Details
   * Enable Positive Pay Sign
   * Enter Cheque Date
   * Enter Amount
6. Click on "Add cheque".

**Note:** Enabling Positive Pay allows cheque clearance without a verification call from IDFC FIRST Bank. 

## Frequently Asked Questions

**How is it securing your payment?**

You provide your cheque details through the app or netbanking. When the beneficiary presents the cheque, the bank verifies the details. This prevents fraud and ensures secure payments. 

**Is it mandatory to use positive pay?**

While not mandatory, IDFC FIRST Bank highly encourages using this feature for maximum cheque security.
