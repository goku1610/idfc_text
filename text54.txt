## Bharat QR payments

Scan & pay, it’s that simple! Time to switch to one of the quickest and easiest payment methods. Bharat QR code is a two-dimensional barcode you will find at merchant outlets or on their website. It contains all the information that you need to make payment to that merchant through your card. 

**Key Features:**

* **Hassle-free on the spot payment**
* **Secured transactions**
* **Quick and convenient process**
* **Scan barcode for quick payments**

## How it works

Experience seamless transactions with Bharat QR payments across various retail outlets. Follow these steps to set it up on your smartphone:

1. Launch your IDFC FIRST Bank Mobile Banking App (latest version).
2. On the login page, tap 'SCAN AND PAY' to activate your phone's camera.
3. Hold the phone camera steady in front of the Bharat QR code and let it scan.
4. Once scanned, the merchant details will auto-populate, and you will need to enter the amount.
5. Proceed with the payment and log in to your mobile banking app when prompted.
6. Select the card you want to use and authorize the transaction with your OTP.
7. Once completed, you and the merchant will receive a confirmation SMS.

**Note:** Transacting with your card at merchant’s websites that accept barcode payments is like swiping your card. It is a completely safe and secure system, and you will be entitled to all the benefits that apply to your card.
