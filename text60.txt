**Transfer funds to 100+ countries in 13 currencies with competitive exchange rates and industry-best transaction fees. Available for both resident and non-resident customers.**

**Here's how easy it is:**

1. **Visit your nearest IDFC FIRST Bank branch.**
2. **Submit the Application for Outward Remittance - [Form A2 (click to download)](/content/dam/IDFCFirstBank/form-center/individual-remittances/Application-for-Outward-Remittance-Form-A2-new.pdf)**

**Frequently Asked Questions:**

* **What is 'Pay Abroad' facility?** 
  'Pay Abroad' allows resident Indians and NRIs to transfer money to beneficiaries outside India (except Nepal and Bhutan) for purposes approved under FEMA. This includes family maintenance, education, gifts, medical treatment, foreign travel, employment abroad, and repatriation of funds.

* **Who is eligible to do a Funds Transfer abroad?**
  Resident and Non-resident account holders of IDFC FIRST Bank with a PAN card linked to their Aadhaar card. NRI customers don't need a PAN card.

* **In which currencies can Fund Transfer abroad be made through IDFC FIRST Bank?**
  USD, EUR, GBP, CAD, AUD, CHF, AED, SGD, HKD, NZD, JPY, ZAR, NOK

* **What is the process of doing Funds transfer abroad?**
  Visit any IDFC FIRST Bank branch and submit the required forms/documents, including the Retail Outward remittance application form (Form A2). [Click here](/content/dam/idfcfirstbank/pdf/Application-for-Outward-Remittance-Form-A2-new.pdf) to download Form A2.

* **Where do I view the exchange rates?**
  [Click here](https://www.idfcfirstbank.com/forex-rates.html) for details on Exchange Rates.
