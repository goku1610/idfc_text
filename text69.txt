## IDFC First Bank - Tax Payments Made Easy

### Online E-Tax Payment

IDFC First Bank simplifies online tax payments, making them quick, easy, and straightforward. 

**To pay your taxes online:**

1.  Use your online banking login credentials.
2.  Choose the relevant tax payment option (Direct Taxes or GST).
3.  Follow the on-screen instructions.

### Pay Direct Taxes

**Follow these steps to pay your direct taxes:**

1.  **Login:** Access the [Income Tax portal](https://www.incometax.gov.in/iec/foportal/) and provide the required details.
2.  **Payment Options:** Select your preferred payment method:
    *   **Payment Gateway:** Pay using your IDFC FIRST Bank Debit/Credit Card or Net Banking.
    *   **NEFT/RTGS:** Generate a Mandate Form on the portal and execute the transfer from your IDFC FIRST Bank account.
3.  **Download Challan:** Once the payment is complete, download the Challan from the Income Tax portal.

### Pay Your GST

**Follow these steps to make your GST payment:**

1.  **Login and Create Challan:**  Go to the [GST portal](https://www.gst.gov.in/) and create a GST Challan with the necessary details.
2.  **Payment Options:** Choose your preferred method:
    *   **Debit Card:**  Refer to the [Debit Card Payment Guide](https://www.idfcfirstbank.com/content/dam/IDFCFirstBank/online-tax-payments/GST-Payment-Debit-Card.pdf) for detailed instructions.
    *   **NEFT/RTGS:**  Review the [NEFT/RTGS Payment Guide](https://www.idfcfirstbank.com/content/dam/IDFCFirstBank/online-tax-payments/GST-Payment-NEFT-RTGS.pdf) for step-by-step guidance.
3.  **Verify Payment:** After successful payment, verify the transaction details on the GST portal.
