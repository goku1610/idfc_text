## Credit Card Bill Payment

Paying your credit card bill online is as easy as swiping the card itself. IDFC FIRST Bank offers multiple convenient options for clearing your credit card bill payment.

**Note:**  Customers are advised to use authorized payment methods to avoid any issues.

## Services

### Net Banking or Debit Card Payments

Make credit card payments through **debit card or net banking** using your IDFC FIRST Bank account.

[Click here to pay](https://my.idfcfirstbank.com/manageCreditcard)

### Internet Banking

**For IDFC FIRST Bank app users:**

1. Go to **Credit Card** > Pay credit card bill after logging in to Internet Banking.
2. Enable 'Autopay future bills' for automatic payments.

**For IDFC FIRST Bank Mobile Banking (Old) app users:**

1. Go to Credit Card > Credit Card Bill after logging in to Internet Banking.

[CLICK HERE TO LOGIN](https://my.idfcfirstbank.com/login)

### UPI Payments

Make instant credit card payments through UPI using your preferred banking app/BHIM app:

1. **Go to UPI section > Send Money**
2. Enter 'your 16-digit card number.cc@idfcbank' as UPI ID.

### IMPS/NEFT Payments

Make instant payments via IMPS/NEFT using these details:

* Payee Name: **Name as on your IDFC FIRST Bank Credit Card**
* Payee Account Number: **Your 16-digit IDFC FIRST Bank Credit Card number**
* Bank Name: **IDFC FIRST Bank**
* IFSC Code: **IDFB0010225**

### Other Payment Options

**Cash Deposit:** Visit your nearest IDFC FIRST Bank branch and deposit cash into your credit card account.

**Cheque:** Drop a cheque in favor of your 16-digit IDFC FIRST Bank Credit Card number at any IDFC FIRST Bank branch/ATM.

### Tokenisation
