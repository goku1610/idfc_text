## Shop. Pay. Repeat.

Redeem exciting rewards with IDFC FIRST Bank Online shopping and payments

### Online shopping payments

Experience the ease of online shopping payment methods with IDFC FIRST Bank. Enjoy our safe and secure channels that help you breeze through your e-commerce shopping. With passcodes sent to your registered mobile number, we offer you a hassle-free shopping experience. 

**IDFC FIRST Bank Debit Card**

Opt for seamless debit card online shopping at select e-commerce websites with an IDFC FIRST Bank Debit Card. Our secure system allows you to either generate an OTP, or enter your Verified by VISA password for each transaction. Here’s how you can carry out online shopping with us:

* Go to your merchant’s website
* Select the product you want to purchase
* Select ‘Debit Card + PIN’ on the payment screen
* Select IDFC FIRST Bank
* Enter your debit card number, expiry date, and CVV
* Confirm the transaction

[Click here](https://www.idfcfirstbank.com/content/dam/IDFCFirstBank/PDF/merchant-list.pdf) to view a detailed list of all merchants where we offer a ‘Debit Card + PIN’ payment option. 

**Net Banking**

Enjoy the ease of online shopping payments with IDFC FIRST Bank NetBanking. Be it shopping, apparel, travel, ticket booking, bill payments, etc., our net banking services let you complete your payments seamlessly. Here’s how you can easily do online shopping through netbanking:

* Go to your merchant’s website
* Select the product you want to purchase
* Select ‘Net Banking’ on the payment screen
* Select IDFC FIRST Bank (you will be redirected to our checkout page)
* Enter your net banking username and password
* Enter the OTP we send to your registered mobile number
* Confirm the transaction

[Click here](https://www.idfcfirstbank.com/content/dam/IDFCFirstBank/PDF/Mercahnts-3rd-March-2017-new.pdf) to find merchants where we offer ‘Net Banking’ as a payment option.
