## Make payments in a jiffy!

Set up your Bharat QR today with IDFC FIRST Bank

### Bharat QR payments

Scan & pay, it's that simple! Time to switch to one of the quickest and easiest payment methods. Bharat QR code is a two-dimensional barcode you will find at merchant outlets or on their website and contain all the information that you need to make payment to that merchant through your card.

**Key Features**

* Hassle-free on-the-spot payment
* Secured transactions
* Quick and convenient process
* Scan barcode for quick payments

### How it works

Experience seamless transactions with Bharat QR payments across various retail outlets. Follow these steps to set it up on your smartphone:

1. Launch your IDFC FIRST Bank Mobile Banking App. Please be sure that you are using the latest version.
2. On the login page itself, you will see a band reading 'SCAN AND PAY'. Simply tapping it will invoke your phone's camera.
3. Hold the phone camera steady in front of the Bharat QR code and let it scan.
4. Once scanned, the merchant details will get auto-populated and you will need to enter the amount.
5. Once you proceed with the payment you will be asked to log in to your mobile banking app.
6. Select the card that you want to transact using and authorize the transaction with your OTP, post logging in.
7. When the transaction is completed, both you and the merchant will receive a confirmation SMS.

**Note:** Transacting with your card at merchant's websites that accept barcode payments is like swiping your card. It is a completely safe and secure system and you will be entitled to all the benefits that apply to your card.
