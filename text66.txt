## Online Shopping Payments with IDFC FIRST Bank

**Shop. Pay. Repeat.** and enjoy exciting rewards with IDFC FIRST Bank's secure online shopping and payment methods.

### Overview

Enjoy hassle-free e-commerce shopping with our safe and secure online payment channels. We offer:

* **Debit Card Payments:** Shop seamlessly on select websites using your IDFC FIRST Bank Debit Card. Our secure system uses OTP or Verified by VISA password for each transaction.
* **Net Banking:** Make payments for a variety of goods and services like shopping, travel, bills, and more with IDFC FIRST Bank NetBanking. Complete transactions smoothly with OTP authentication on our secure checkout page.
