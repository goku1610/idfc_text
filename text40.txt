## Insurance through Savings Account with IDFC FIRST Bank

**Pradhan Mantri Insurance Solutions**

This page details the benefits of Pradhan Mantri Insurance plans available through IDFC FIRST Bank. These plans are linked to your savings account for easy premium payment.

**Key Benefits:**

* **Social Security Schemes:** Access social security schemes under PM Jan Dhan Yojana.
* **Life Cover:** Get up to ₹2 lakh life cover with PMJJBY.
* **Accident Cover:** Avail up to ₹2 lakh accident cover with PMSBY.
* **Accessibility:** Available for all savings bank account holders.

**Pradhan Mantri Suraksha Bima Yojana (PMSBY)**

* **Coverage:** Accidental death and disability cover up to ₹2 lakh (₹1 lakh for partial disability).
* **Premium:** ₹20 per annum.
* **Eligibility:** Savings account holders between 18 and 70 years of age.
* **Payment:** Premium auto-debited from your savings account.

**[Apply Now](https://www.idfcfirstbank.com/pmsby)**  
**[COI / Policy Download](https://ilhc.icicilombard.com/coi/coicertificate)** 

**Pradhan Mantri Jeevan Jyoti Bima Yojana (PMJJBY)**

* **Coverage:** Life insurance coverage up to ₹2 lakh for death due to any reason.
* **Premium:** ₹436 per annum.
* **Eligibility:** Savings account holders between 18 and 50 years of age.
* **Payment:** Premium auto-debited from your savings account.

**[Apply Now](https://www.idfcfirstbank.com/pmjjby)**
